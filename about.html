About Me.

Ethan Byrd | UNC Chapel Hill

Hi, I'm Ethan, a software engineer, basketball nerd, and proud Tar Heel from Clemmons, NC.

I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where I studied Computer Science with a minor in Data Science. During my time there, I developed a passion for turning data into decisions and building software that solves real problems.

My work spans full-stack web development, database design, and data science. I'm especially passionate about working with and analysing real performance data to improve decision-making in athletics. Whether it's developing a user-friendly frontend experience, writing complex SQL queries, or evaluating second-round prospects, you're going to get 110% out of me.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me deep in NBA box scores, training for a 5k, or watching Carolina play.
